## Detailed Notes

- Covers ethical foundations and operational guidelines for responsible AI in public-sector settings.
- Structures delivery around process-based governance, stakeholder impact assessment, and implementation accountability.
- Includes guidance on transparency and interpretability trade-offs, with emphasis on context-specific model choice.
- Highlights reliability, security, and robustness as safety-critical requirements, not optional enhancements.
