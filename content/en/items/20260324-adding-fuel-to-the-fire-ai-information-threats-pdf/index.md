## Detailed Notes

- Proposes an AI information-threat taxonomy across three stages: content generation, dissemination, and user engagement during crisis events.
- Identifies concrete risk vectors including deceptive synthetic media, social-bot amplification networks, AI-powered aggregation distortions, and chatbot-enabled narrative escalation.
- Also documents defensive applications: harmful-network detection, rapid fact-checking amplification, and coordinated cross-platform response playbooks.
- Recommends government and industry crisis protocols with clearer accountability, rehearsal mechanisms, and transparency on system limitations.
