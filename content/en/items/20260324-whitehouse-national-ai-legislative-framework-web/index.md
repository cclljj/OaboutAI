## Detailed Notes

- Presents six policy pillars: child/parent protections, community and energy safeguards, IP and creator rights, free speech protections, innovation enablement, and workforce development.
- Stresses federal leadership and uniform national rules to avoid a fragmented compliance environment across states.
- Positions AI policy as both national-competitiveness strategy and domestic trust-building agenda.
- Signals near-term legislative engagement with Congress to formalize framework elements into statute.
