## Detailed Notes

- The core issue is the speed mismatch between grassroots AI adoption and formal governance cycles.
- Shadow AI expands when teams prioritize convenience and speed over approved toolchains.
- Governance blind spots can expose proprietary, customer, or regulated data to uncontrolled processing.
- Risk is amplified when model outputs influence decisions without validation or human review standards.
- Traditional procurement/control processes are too slow for high-velocity AI tool onboarding.
- Effective governance needs continuous visibility into which tools are actually used in practice.
- Policy must be translated into enforceable controls, not only awareness guidance.
- Accountability should map AI usage to owners, risk levels, and escalation procedures.
- Cross-functional coordination (IT, security, legal, operations) is required to close control gaps.
- Sustainable AI adoption depends on balancing experimentation speed with auditable safeguards.
